## How to Play  
1. Click on **Rock**, **Paper**, or **Scissors**.  
2. The computer makes its choice.  
3. The winner is determined based on classic rules:  
   - Rock beats Scissors  
   - Scissors beat Paper  
   - Paper beats Rock  
4. The score updates accordingly.
